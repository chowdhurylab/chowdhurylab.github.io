Filtered Enzyme Parameter Dataset

The enzyme kinetic parameters kcat and Km were systematically curated from BRENDA (release 2023_1) and SABIO-RK (as of May 2024) using custom Python scripts, resulting in a initial dataset of 30,442 kcat and 44,615 Km entries. After filtering for maximum kcat and minimum Km values, the dataset was further cleaned and merged with substrate SMILES from PubChem and sequence data from UniProt and BRENDA. This process yielded 26,244 entries with both kcat and Km values, including 10,244 mutant entries, sourced from 2,158 scientific articles with PubMed IDs. A thorough and manual curation resolved inconsistencies, expanding the mutant dataset to 11,178 entries and ensuring data accuracy. The final dataset, comprising 16,000 wild-type and 11,178 mutant entries, serves as a high-quality, error-free resource for training machine learning models to predict kinetic parameters for novel enzymes.
